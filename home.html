What is Onimari Creatives?

Onimari Creatives was born out of a simple realization: to be a student today means creating everything with a professional touch-from class presentations to digital portfolios-and from there to juggling the full weight of subjects, deadlines, and other responsibilities.

We know what it is to deal with constant pressure from every kind of deadline, for great-looking visuals that attract any audience, and for a longing to be noticed even if he or she may not have the professional design skills to pull it off. With the creativity of a student and the vision of a designer, I spotted the gap that stands—the realization that not everyone will have time to design from scratch, not all will have the skill to do it, not everyone will have the right tools, and of course, not everybody can afford premium services. This is the reason why Onimari Creatives was set up.
