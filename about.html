About Us

Company History

Onimari Creatives is founded primarily to provide students access to beautiful editable Canva templates to help in saving time and enriching the presentation. This was originally a side project for sharing my templates but quickly transformed into a growing platform, where the students can access aesthetic, functional, and easy-to-use designs for every school need.

What Sets Us Apart From Others?

While some generic template provider, Onimari Creatives, a student-to-student user design by a student. Every template is formed based on academics, considering whether it be for class reports, portfolios, presentations, or resumes. Our designs are:

  • Affordable (or free)
  • Aesthetics, yet Practical
  • Optimized for Canva (No design experience required)
  • For Real Academic Use Cases
